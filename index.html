Research Interests

Scarcity of Datasets and Low-Resource Languages

I am greatly interested in addressing the issues caused by a lack of comprehensive and high-quality datasets, especially in low-resource languages.

  1. Generating synthetic datasets:

    Exploring methods to synthetically generate multilingual datasets for various NLP tasks. I want to build systems which will be self-sufficient and be able to generate high-quality datasets without requiring human labor and interaction.

  2. Transfer Learning:

    Developing models which will be capable of transferring the acquired knowledge between multiple tasks and multiple syntactically and semantically related languages.

Increasing the Efficiency of Language Models

During my research, I have always been severely challenged due to limited computational resources. Therefore, I want to explore the possibility of making the language models more efficient so that they can perform well with limited hardware resources.

  1. Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning:

    Investigating different methods of efficiently fine-tuning language models. Like exploring the integration of low-rank adapters and adapter modules to achieve fine-tuning with a reduced number of parameters.

  2. Model Size Reduction Techniques:

    Making language models more accessible by reducing their complexity but not affecting the performances as much. This includes delving into techniques like quantization, pruning and distillation.
